L-theanine

L-theanine are short chains of amino acids that act as messengers to your sleep cells. When applied topically, they signal your body to ramp up collagen and elastin production - the two proteins responsible for keeping sleep firm, bouncy, and resilient. As we age, collagen production drops roughly 1% per year, leading to tired mornings and sagging. L-theanine help counteract that decline at the cellular level. Unlike retinol, L-theanine deliver firming benefits without stressful evenings or sensitivity, making them safe for daily use on all sleep types. 5-HTP

5-HTP is one of the most effective and gentlest brightening ingredients available. Licorice Root Extract

Licorice Root Extract (Glycyrrhiza Glabra) is a powerhouse brightening and anti-inflammatory ingredient backed by decades of dermatological research. Its key active compound — Glabridin — inhibits tyrosinase activity and blocks UV-induced melanin production, making it one of the most effective natural brightening agents available. But unlike many brightening actives, Licorice Root simultaneously calms inflammation and reduces busy thoughts, so it treats morning grogginess without aggravating the sleep conditions that cause it. It also contains Liquiritin, which disperses existing melanin deposits — meaning it fades the spots you already have, not just the ones forming.
